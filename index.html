About Us

Welcome to the Greene Garden “Greene with an E”

Nestled in the heart of Fort Greene, Brooklyn, the Greene Garden is a vibrant, member-run community space rooted in wellness, education, and environmental stewardship.

As part of the Brooklyn Queens Land Trust, we nurture a green oasis where neighbors gather for events and build deeper connections with one another and the land. From youth programming and wellness classes to seasonal celebrations and volunteer days, the Greene Garden is where community grows.

Summer Internship

Week 1

Interns got to know each other and brainstormed ideas for their garden projects.

Week 2

Interns took trips to green spaces, learning soil and water science while practicing citizen science.

Week 3

Interns got hands-on experience on garden visits to local community gardens where they collaborated to help them out.

Week 4

Interns explored their environment by learning to identify trees, diagnose plants, and taking birding walks in Fort Greene Park!

Week 5

Interns were stewards of plant life in the Greene Garden, completing tree care and soil checkups on home turf while working on their project presentations.

Week 6

Interns presented their hard work on their garden projects to peers, educators, and industry professionals who they met over the course of the program!
